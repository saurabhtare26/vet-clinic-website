Who We Are

Your Pets' Health is Our Priority

Founded in 2012, Paws & Care Veterinary Clinic has been serving the pet community in Malad, Mumbai with warmth and clinical excellence. Our team of qualified vets and support staff treat every animal as their own.

We combine modern diagnostic equipment with a calm, welcoming environment so both you and your pet feel comfortable from the moment you walk in.

  • ✅ IAVM Certified Veterinarians
  • ✅ In-house diagnostic lab
  • ✅ Separate dog & cat waiting areas
  • ✅ Cashless pet insurance accepted
