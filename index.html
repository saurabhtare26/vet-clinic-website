Meet the Team

Led by Passion,
Guided by Science

Founded in 2012, Paws & Care was built on a simple belief: every animal deserves the same standard of medical care as any human patient. Dr. Meera Nair and her team of six veterinarians bring together over 60 years of combined experience.

Our clinic is equipped with a fully sterile operation theatre, an in-house diagnostic laboratory, digital radiography, and a dedicated isolation ward for infectious cases — all in a calm, pet-friendly environment designed to reduce stress and anxiety.
